About Planti Dati

Welcome to our unique gardening platform, the definitive destination for green thumbs in Ireland.

Our Mission

This interactive, user-driven platform is designed to bridge the gap between experienced horticulturists and those just beginning their journey in the world of plants.

Our platform stands as the first of its kind to harness the voluntary contributions of growers from various locations, offering a comprehensive repository of invaluable plant-growing knowledge and insights.

Core Features

The core features of our platform include an expansive, searchable database filled with detailed information about a myriad of plant varieties. This resource serves as a living compendium of plant knowledge, enabling users to research and select plants that best suit their needs and environments.

For a more personalized experience, registered users can create their own calendars. By providing insights into plant growth in different conditions and regions, these calendars contribute to our collective knowledge base.

Join Our Growing Family

As a project rooted in community and the passion for gardening, we aim to continually evolve and expand based on our user's needs. We invite you to join us in this green endeavour, whether you're a seasoned horticulturist, a budding gardener, or simply curious about the world of plants.
